Sandwich

Description

Because I know nothing about food, I keep the description short

Ingredients

  • 4 slices Italian bread
  • 4 slices ripe tomato
  • 4 leaves fresh basil
  • 4 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Lay 4 slices of Italian bread out on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Top each piece of bread with a tomato slice, a basil leaf, and a slice of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese has melted, about 7 minutes.
  6. Drizzle each sandwich with 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
